INSTRUCTIONS

READ ALL INSTRUCTIONS BEFORE USING THE APPLIANCE! MISINTERPRETING OR FAILING TO FOLLOW THE PROCEDURE MAY RESULT IN PROPERTY DAMAGE, PERSONAL INJURY, OR DEATH!


1.Choose the perfect location

Step 1:

Choose an open space

Do not use outdoors

Step 2:

Do not put on rugs or carpets. Place it on a firm flat level surface. Under the unit put a mat or pad.

Step 3:

Do not put in a room where it will be accessible to children; where you sleep or rest.

Step 4:

Use a hygrometer to check the humidity in the room. DO not use in rooms with humidity level less than 50%.

Step 5:

Do not place it near heat sources or under overhanging sources.

2.The right position is important too...

Step 1:

Locate the humidifier on an inside wall near an electrical outlet. Should be at least 4 inches(10cm) away from the wall for best results.

Step 2:

Put the appliance directly into a 120V AC electrical outlet.

Step 3:

Do not allow the moisture outlet to directly face the wall

3.Take care of the cord

Step 1:

Keep the cord out of heavy traffic areas

Step 2:

Do not place the cord under rugs or near heat registers

Step 3:

Do not use if the cord or the plug are damaged. Return it immediately or ask for repair.

4.When refilling:

Step 1:

Unplug it. Be sure to pull on the plug, not the cord

Step 2:

Do not pour water in any opening other than the water tank

Step 3:

Do not move it until it cools completely

Step 4:

Use both hands when carrying full tank of water

5.While working

Step 1:

Do not place anything over a moisture outlet

Step 2:

Never operate unit without water inside

Step 3:

Do not tilt, move or attempt to empty while working. Shut it off and move the unit.

6.Prohibited operations:
